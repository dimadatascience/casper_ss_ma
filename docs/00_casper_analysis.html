Objective

In the context of the CD82 protein project, which has been linked to both therapy response and aneuploidy levels, we aim to validate these correlations in a patient cohort. To achieve this, we performed RNA sequencing on samples from:

  • 50 triple-negative breast cancer (TNBC) patients (characterized by high aneuploidy),
  • 50 hormone receptor–positive (HR+) breast cancer patients (characterized by moderate aneuploidy), and
  • 50 healthy controls.

Our objective is to investigate whether a correlation exists between CD82 expression levels and aneuploidy scores in these patients. The goal of this report is to determine the aneuploidy score using the RNA-seq data. CaSpER

Introduction

The RNA-seq data were analyzed using the Illumina DRAGEN Bio-IT platform (version 4.2.4), with the hg38 genome and Gencode v46 transcriptome reference. We followed the protocol detailed in this guide to compute the aneuploidy scores. At the moment we have 49 healthy donor, 50 HR+ patients and 50 TNBC patients.
